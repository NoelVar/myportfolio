Projects

Students as Co-Creators Project (CODEMATICS)

[02/2023 - 07/2023]

Participated in a project along with other students and teachers to bridge the gap between level 4 Mathematics and Programming. We held a workshop to gather data from students and academic staff through the usage of surveys. Using Protégé we also created ontologies to represent the connections between the two subjects, which was shown at the workshop. The collected data was processed and used to finalise the ontology and create our final report. Our team has also created a website which provides Mathematical and Programming example to try to bridge the gap between the two subjects. The final report was written collectively and contains multiple data-tables. GitHub: HERE

For this project I got to work as a hired staff at University of Westminster.
